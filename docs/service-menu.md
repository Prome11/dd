# Service Menu

This menu keeps offers clear enough for a first inquiry. It is designed for small paid service orders that can be delivered quickly and improved through repeat work.

## 1. PPT Fast Polish

**For:** students, employees, founders, or operators who already have rough materials but need a clearer deck.

**Inputs needed:**
- Topic and audience
- Deadline
- Existing PPT, Word document, notes, or outline
- Preferred style if any

**Deliverables:**
- Slide structure rewrite
- Page-by-page copy suggestions
- Visual and layout checklist
- Final delivery notes for revision

**Reference price:** RMB 199-699

**Good fit:** work report, year-end report, defense deck, product intro, project summary.

## 2. Xiaohongshu Content Starter

**For:** service sellers who need content that can attract inquiries without sounding like spam.

**Inputs needed:**
- Service type
- Target customer
- Existing account or positioning notes
- Proof points, cases, or available resources

**Deliverables:**
- Account positioning summary
- 10 post ideas
- 3 polished post drafts
- CTA and inquiry wording
- Simple 7-day posting plan

**Reference price:** RMB 299-899

**Good fit:** PPT services, document services, local business services, operator services, consulting services.

## 3. Home Live Ops Kit

**For:** home-living products, livestream teams, or creators who need better sales language and live-room structure.

**Inputs needed:**
- Product name and category
- Target customer
- Product specs, price, photos, and current pain points
- Existing livestream script if any

**Deliverables:**
- Product selling-point map
- 30-60 minute livestream script
- Objection-handling Q&A bank
- Comment reply snippets
- Operator checklist

**Reference price:** RMB 699-2999

**Good fit:** lighting, furniture, storage, textile, decor, renovation-adjacent products.

## 4. Custom Research + Delivery

**For:** unclear business problems that require diagnosis before writing.

**Inputs needed:**
- Current goal
- Current bottleneck
- Public references or anonymized examples
- Decision deadline

**Deliverables:**
- Diagnosis memo
- Action plan
- Templates or copy assets
- Delivery checklist

**Reference price:** quote after scope.

## Revision Rules

- Small copy edits: included once or twice depending on package.
- Major direction changes: treated as a new scope.
- Sensitive/private material: should be shared through a private channel, not public GitHub issues.
- Rush orders: quote separately.

## Quote Formula

Quote = base package + material complexity + deadline pressure + revision depth + research depth.

For a first order, keep the scope small. A paid diagnostic or one-page rewrite is often better than a vague large project.
