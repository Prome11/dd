## 30-Minute Script Structure

1. Opening hook: 1-2 minutes
2. Product scene: 3-5 minutes
3. Pain point explanation: 3 minutes
4. Feature demonstration: 8-10 minutes
5. Objection handling: 5 minutes
6. Offer and urgency: 3 minutes
7. Recap and next product transition: 2 minutes

## Comment Reply Bank

- "Is it easy to clean?"
- "Will it fit a small apartment?"
- "Is the color accurate?"
- "Can it be installed by one person?"
- "What is the difference from the cheaper one?"
- "Can I return it?"

## Operator Checklist

- Product sample ready
- Price and coupon confirmed
- Key specs printed
- Common objections prepared
- Camera close-up path tested
- Comment keywords assigned
- After-live notes recorded
