# Delivery Quality Checklist

Use this before sending any paid delivery.

## Scope Check

- Deliverables match the quote.
- Deadline is still accurate.
- Revision count is documented.
- Sensitive information is removed from public examples.

## PPT Check

- Every page has one main point.
- Titles are conclusion-style, not vague labels.
- Page order tells a clear story.
- Font size is readable.
- Colors are consistent.
- Charts have clear labels.
- PDF preview exported.

## Content Check

- The target customer is clear.
- Claims are specific and truthful.
- CTA is natural.
- The post does not rely on fake screenshots or fake income claims.
- The first paid step is easy to understand.

## Livestream Script Check

- Opening hook fits the product.
- Selling points are tied to real specs or usage scenes.
- Objections have short replies.
- Offer wording is clear.
- Operator notes are practical.

## Final Delivery Message

Hi, this is the first delivery for the agreed scope:

- Files included:
- What changed:
- What to review first:
- Included revision:
- Please avoid sending private material in public channels:

Thank you.
