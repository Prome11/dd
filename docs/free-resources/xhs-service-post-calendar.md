# Xiaohongshu Service Post Calendar

This 7-day plan is for ethical service promotion. Use real examples, clear limitations, and truthful wording.

## Positioning Rule

One account should make three things clear:

- Who you help
- What problem you solve
- What the first paid step looks like

## 7-Day Calendar

| Day | Post Type | Topic | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Problem post | Why most PPT orders become messy | Ask for a brief template |
| 2 | Checklist | 12 things to confirm before PPT production | Save the checklist |
| 3 | Before/after structure | Rough outline vs clear outline | Ask for a structure audit |
| 4 | Service menu | What I can help with and what I do not do | Open an inquiry |
| 5 | Mini case | A presentation structure rewritten from messy notes | Ask for similar rewrite |
| 6 | FAQ | How pricing, revisions, and deadlines work | Ask for a quote |
| 7 | Free resource | PPT client brief template | Download and request help |

## CTA Examples

- Comment "brief" if you want the intake template.
- Send a non-sensitive summary if you want a fixed-scope quote.
- If your deadline is close, share page count and current material status first.

## Compliance Notes

- Do not claim guaranteed income or guaranteed traffic.
- Do not use fake customer screenshots.
- Do not expose client documents.
- Do not pretend to be a neutral reviewer if you are selling the service.
