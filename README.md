This repository is a public service hub for practical AI-assisted business support. It focuses on clear deliverables, transparent scope, and reusable templates.

## What This Helps With

- Turn rough notes, documents, and screenshots into clear PPT outlines, slide copy, or polished presentation assets.
- Build Xiaohongshu content plans, service posts, and simple lead-generation copy.
- Create home-living livestream scripts, product selling points, comment-response scripts, and operator SOPs.
- Standardize client intake, delivery checks, revision boundaries, and reusable templates.

## How To Request Help

Open a service inquiry here:

[Create a Service Inquiry](../../issues/new?template=service-inquiry.yml)

Please do not post private company documents, personal IDs, passwords, payment information, unpublished business secrets, or customer data in a public issue. Share only a non-sensitive summary first.

## Operating Principles

- Real work only: no fake cases, fake income claims, fake testimonials, or misleading before/after results.
- Clear scope: every order should define deliverables, deadline, revision count, and source material.
- Privacy first: sensitive client materials stay out of public repositories.
- Useful artifacts: every delivery should leave behind reusable templates, checklists, or examples.

## Suggested First Order

If you are unsure where to start, request a small diagnostic:

1. Send a non-sensitive description of your goal.
2. Share the audience, deadline, and current material status.
3. Ask for one of three outputs: PPT outline, content plan, or livestream script.
4. Receive a fixed-scope quote before production begins.
